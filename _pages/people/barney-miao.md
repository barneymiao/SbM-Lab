## Biography

Dr. Barney H. Miao leads the Smart Building Materials (SbM) Lab, focusing on sustainable, intelligent, and circular construction materials.

His research integrates bio-based materials, artificial intelligence, non-destructive sensing, and computational modeling to develop next-generation infrastructure materials.
